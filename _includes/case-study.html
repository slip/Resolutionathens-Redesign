Case Study
Sorba Atlanta

Discover

Initially, Brett wanted a straight redesign. The site is essentially a blogging platform for their organization and he wanted it looking good.

As we went through the discovery phase, we identified our primary goals as unifying the look and feel of the brand across platforms, allowing easy updates through Wordpress, enhancing usability through restructuring of information, increasing awareness of the organization, generating mobile traffic by making the site responsive, boosting direct traffic, attracting repeat visitors, and generating donations and new membership.

Concept and Design

I began taking a close look at the sites of other Sorba chapters, trying to glean what information was most important to Sorba members. It turned out to be trail status - mountain bikers want to know if they can ride. I also wanted to draw attention to the donate button and keep the homepage fresh by featuring recent blog posts.

I provided Brett with a couple of style tiles - small, flexible documents to communicate the visual evolution of the brand.

Architecture

Once I had an idea of our design direction, I created documents for sitemap, color palette, and font choices.

Wireframes

I provided detailed wireframes for all pages of the site. I looked for approval and improvement with each step forward.

Build

Once I had approval on our wireframes, I gave flesh to their bones and wireframes became comps. I used the wonderful Invision app to make these comps functional, allowing Brett to get a feel for the flow of the site.

Turning pretty pictures into a working website is no small task. I sat down with my favorite text editor and built a custom WordPress theme from scratch, making sure that every pixel was in its place and that my code was clean and semantic. The final site can be found here.

Evolve

We are working with Brett and the board at Sorba Atlanta to provide WordPress training, including the use of the Give donation plugin. We are also providing them with a content calendar for regular updates, creating campaigns for email and social media, and setting up Google Analytics to monitor the success of our continuing efforts.
